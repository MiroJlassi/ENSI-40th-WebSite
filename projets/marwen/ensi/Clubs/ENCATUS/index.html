ABOUT US

Enactus is a global non-profit organization that brings together students, academic professionals, and business leaders to address pressing social and environmental challenges through entrepreneurial action. Enactus aims to empower communities and transform lives by leveraging the power of entrepreneurship and innovative thinking. Enactus projects often focus on areas such as economic empowerment, environmental sustainability, and social inclusion, striving to achieve tangible results that benefit both local communities and the world at large.
Enactus ENSI, founded in 2021, is actively engaged in two impactful projects: Indimej and TremoEase, both of which showcase the organization's dedication to addressing pressing social and environmental challenges through entrepreneurial action. This year, Enactus ENSI is gearing up to showcase the outcomes of these projects with its second participation in the Enactus Tunisia National Exposition, where they will have the opportunity to demonstrate their commitment to creating positive change and sustainable impact in their community and beyond. By participating in this prestigious competition with one of their projects, Enactus ENSI reaffirms its role as a catalyst for social innovation and entrepreneurship in Tunisia.
