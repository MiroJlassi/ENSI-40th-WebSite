ABOUT US

The ENSI Music Club, founded in the 90s, is one of the pillars of associative life at the national school of computer science Through the organization of cultural activities and musical events it encourages students to express their passion for music and showcase their talents

The mission of the music club is to promote and celebrate music as an art and form of expression. We offer an inclusive space for music enthusiasts to share their interests, develop their musical and social skills and express their creativity...
