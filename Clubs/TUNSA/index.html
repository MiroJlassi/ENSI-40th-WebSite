ABOUT US

TUNSA is an abbreviation of the Tunisian Space Association. The purpose of this association is to contribute to the advancement of astronomy in Tunisia. In 2023, the association took the initiative to form clubs within universities Tunisia to facilitate access to this field for students.

Established on January 23, 2024, the TUNSA ENSI Club serves as a vibrant hub where space enthusiasts converge to nurture their curiosity and fervor for the cosmos. Our core objective is to empower ENSI's students with practical experience and profound insights into space, fostering Tunisia's progress in the global space arena. Committed to inclusivity and collaboration, we aspire to create a welcoming environment where individuals from diverse backgrounds can immerse themselves in the captivating expanse of space, driven by a shared passion to learn, explore, and exchange ideas.
