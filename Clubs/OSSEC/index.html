ABOUT US

OSSEC (open Source Software Ensi Club) is a club inaugurated at the National School of Computer Sciences founded in 2012. Our Club is involved in activities and diverse events. Our mission is to spread the open source philosophy in the community engineering students as well as the organization of events around this field. Our goal is to promote the Open Source culture among ENSI students, which is essentially based on the exchange and sharing of knowledge.
