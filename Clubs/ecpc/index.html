ABOUT US

The ENSI Competitive Programming Club, founded in 2017, is a club whose main activities revolve around competitive programming.
We organize training sessions in algorithms, data structures, and optimization techniques for problem-solving, as well as online and offline competitions, such as our event EPC.

Our community is the only one on the Manouba campus responsible for these activities, aiming to prepare as many students as possible to compete in the Tunisian Collegiate Programming Contest (TCPC).
For more than 5 consecutive years, teams from our community have been ranked in the top 10 at TCPC.
