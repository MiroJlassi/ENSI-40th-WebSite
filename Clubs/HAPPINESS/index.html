ABOUT US

The happiness club, founded in 2022 within the national school of computer science ENSI, the Happiness club is committed to cultivating the happiness and well-being of the entire school community Through a variety of activities, including educational entertainment, relaxation sessions, mentoring programs and social events

The club aspires to create an inclusive environment where each member, whether student, member of administrative or other staff, feels supported, fulfilled and connected. By working closely with all school stakeholders, the HAPPINESS club aims to create an atmosphere positive and stimulating that promotes academic success, personal development and the overall well-being of each person.
