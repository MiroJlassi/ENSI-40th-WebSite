ABOUT US

Founded in 2006, ENSI Junior Enterprise is a non-profit association aimed at introducing Tunisian students to entrepreneurial life.
This is achieved through the development of activities based on three main pillars: training, events, and project realization in the field of ICT.

Our association has always been determined to carve its path of excellence, innovation, and expertise as one of the pioneers of the Tunisian Junior Enterprises movement while embracing the entrepreneurial spirit in its work.
ENSI Junior Enterprise is an organization committed to the values of its members and a socio-economic actor, national, committed, and sustainable.
Our mission is to gradually integrate young students into an entrepreneurial environment through concrete experiences ensuring their skills development.
