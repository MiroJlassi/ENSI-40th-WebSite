ABOUT US

The ENSI robotics association is a non-profit association, founded in 2006 by a group of student engineers who are amateurs in robotics, accompanied by the supervision of Professor Loncef Tagina, with the aim of immersing computer scientists in the world of robotics with the aim of sharpening their skills and realizing their creativity.
