ABOUT US

Co’ART is short for collaborative art.
we collaborate with our fellow ENSI clubs for their events and we’re all about promoting creativity.
We believe that everyone has a little artist in them. Therefore, we provide a suitable space for all club members to freely express their passion and creativity.
Our activities involve two main aspects: collaborating with other ENSI clubs and hosting internal workshops such as calligraphy, painting, coloring and crafting workshops.
