ABOUT US

Citoyens Positifs et Sociables is a club founded in 2011 within ENSI, supported by the regional committee of the Tunisian Red Crescent in Manouba. It is in cooperation with trustworthy civil society associations and organizations.

Our events are the perfect opportunity to connect, explore nature and do good. Join us for outdoor adventures, charitable efforts and cozy times around the campfire.
